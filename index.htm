Narnia - A place to visit this summer

Narnia is a mid-sized town located in the beautiful country of sweden.
It is well known for its calm and serene environment.
We recieve more than 50,000 tourists visitng us every year during the summer.
This beautiful laid back town is home to 5000 families.
Population 23,000 Residents
We have two schools, five parks, one lake, two shopping places and two cinema theatres along with sports arena.
The local food is delicious and is served in 10 restaurents scattered all over the town.
